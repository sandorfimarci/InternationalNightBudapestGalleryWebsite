Lake Balaton

Balaton is the biggest natural lake in central europe. Lake Balaton, or the “Hungarian sea” as the locals call it, is the number one summer-getaway destination in Hungary. It is a well known destination for germans too, because of historical backgrounds.
Northern shore of Lake Balaton

Before the collapse of the Berlin Wall, Lake Balaton was already one of the favorite destinations among East German holidaymakers, a tradition that’s passed down till this day. No restaurateur around the lake would hire a waiter/waitress who doesn’t have some German language skills.

Sziget festival

Sziget festival is one of the largest music and cultural festivals in Europe. More than 1,000 performances take place each year. E.g.: Ed Shreeran, Foo Fighters, Post Malone, 21 pilots, Macklemore, Martin Garrix, David Guetta, Shawn Mendes
Balaton Sound festival

Balaton Sound is one of Europe's largest open air electronic music festivals with more than 165,000 attending based on the shore of Lake Balaton.

Foods

Chimney cake or "Kürtőskalács"

Kürtőskalács is a spit cake specific to Transylvania, Hungary. Kürtőskalács is made from sweet, yeast dough (raised dough), of which a strip is spun and then wrapped around a truncated cone–shaped baking spit, and rolled in granulated sugar.
Langosh or "Lángos"

Langosh is a Hungarian food speciality, a deep fried dough. It is a typical hungarian "fastfood". You can buy it in Wasen or in Octoberfest too.
Rétes - An authentic pastry

What is it: A strudel-shaped pastry filled with a sugared fruit paste of apple or cherry.
What does it taste like: Generally served along with coffee, these may look small but are very fulfilling. The stuffing is creamy and sticky! Yum!
Kakaos Csiga – A chocolate roll

What is it: A spiral-shaped dough resembling a cinnamon roll swirled with chocolate.
What does it taste like: The sweet cake blends in the mouth with the creamy chocolate filling.
Mádartej ("Bird milk") – A popular dessert

What is it: Vanilla custard is elaborately covered with fresh meringue.
What does it taste like: If there were some things that money can’t buy, then it would surely be the aroma and taste of this dessert. Aphrodisiac!
